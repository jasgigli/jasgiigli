Since our inception, we have been committed to providing quality education to our students. Our journey began with a vision to create a nurturing environment where students could excel academically and grow into responsible citizens.

Over the years, we have consistently produced top-performing students who have gone on to achieve great success in their academic and professional endeavors.

Our Mission

At New Islamia Public High School, our mission is to provide a holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of our students. We strive to instill in them a lifelong love for learning, a sense of responsibility, and a commitment to making a positive impact in the world.
